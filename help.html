Editing

  • Click the Add to the Queue button to add a new task to your list.
  • On the new task, double click on the task name to rename it and either hit enter or click the save changes link.
  • Double click on the type to get a dropdown list of the available activity types.
  • Right click anywhere on the task to reveal the details associated with the task; right click again on the details or the task and it will hide itself.
  • On the details panel, double click in the area in between the panel title and the Complete Task button to add details similarly to task renaming.
  • Tasks can be reordered easily by dragging the task up and down in the list.

Working With

  • Click the Start button to start working on a task.
  • The active task will have a red border and its button will change to a Pause button.
  • Only one task can be active at a time, this makes sense since, while you can multi-task, you are not actually doing work on 2 different tasks at the same time.
  • Click on the Complete Task button in the details pane to mark the task complete and remove it from your list.

Un-completing Tasks

  • If you have marked a task complete, and then get more requests for that task at a later time, you can "un-complete" a task.
  • Click on the Completed Tasks button on the button of the screen to show a list of you 10 most recently completed tasks. Each time you click the Completed Tasks button you will cycle through 10 tasks at a time.
  • Once you find the task you wish to un-complete, click the button with the unlock icon next to the task name and it will be added back into your tasklist.
  • Click the Hide Completes button to hide your completed tasks.

Salesforce Sync

  • A direct linkage is not currently implemented, so you will need to manually input your times into salesforce.
  • Your time spent on tasks will be displayed in a table on the left side of the page. This time is displayed in an hourly value that is directly usable by Salesforce.
  • You will need to update Salesforce daily as the time spent on tasks is cumulative until reset with the reset button.
  • After importing your time into Salesforce, click Open Menu and Reset the daily totals to erase the stored time for that day.

Manager View

  • In this view you can see both Technical Guru's tasklists side-by-side.
  • You can add tasks to either list, reorder lists, and drag tasks between lists.
  • If you leave the Manager View open too long, it may become out of sync with the current tasks. To update the tasks, click on Open Menu and Refresh each user's list.
